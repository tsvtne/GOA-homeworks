All About Cacti

Introduction

Cacti are succulent plants known for their ability to store water in their thick, fleshy tissues. They are often found in arid regions and can survive in harsh conditions.

Types of Cacti

  • Saguaro Cactus
  • Barrel Cactus
  • Prickly Pear Cactus
  • Cholla Cactus
  • Organ Pipe Cactus

Interesting Facts About Cacti

  • Cacti can store water for months, allowing them to survive in desert environments.
  • Some cacti can grow up to 50 feet tall, such as the Saguaro cactus.
  • Cacti have specialized spines to protect them from animals and to reduce water loss.
  • Flowers of many cacti bloom only for a short period, typically at night.
