Eagle Facts

Introduction

Eagles are large, powerful birds of prey known for their keen eyesight and majestic flight. They are found all over the world, especially in North America.

Interesting Facts About Eagles

  • Eagles have excellent vision, which allows them to spot prey from high in the sky.
  • They can fly at speeds of up to 100 mph in some cases.
  • Eagles are known to build the largest nests of any bird species.
  • The Bald Eagle is the national bird of the United States.
