All About Bacteria

Introduction

Bacteria are microscopic, single-celled organisms that can be found in almost every environment on Earth. They are one of the oldest forms of life and play essential roles in various biological processes.

Types of Bacteria

  • Cocci (spherical shaped)
  • Bacilli (rod shaped)
  • Spirilla (spiral shaped)
  • Vibrios (comma shaped)

Interesting Facts About Bacteria

  • Bacteria are among the most abundant organisms on Earth.
  • They can survive in extreme conditions, such as in boiling water or high radiation environments.
  • Some bacteria are essential for human health, aiding in digestion and immunity.
  • Antibiotics are used to treat bacterial infections, but resistance is becoming a growing concern.
