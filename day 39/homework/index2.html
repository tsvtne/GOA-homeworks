All About Alligators

Introduction

Alligators are large reptiles that are native to the southeastern United States and China. They are known for their powerful jaws and stealthy behavior in the wild.

Types of Alligators

  • American Alligator
  • Chinese Alligator

Interesting Facts About Alligators

  • Alligators have a lifespan of 35 to 50 years in the wild.
  • They are carnivores and will eat fish, birds, and small mammals.
  • Alligators can grow up to 13-15 feet long, depending on the species.
  • They communicate using bellows, growls, and other vocalizations.
